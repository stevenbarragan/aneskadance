Studio Rules

Please review our studio guidelines before your class

Arrive Early

Please arrive 10-5 minutes before your scheduled class time to check in and prepare. This helps ensure classes start on time and gives you a chance to warm up properly.

Dress Code

Wear comfortable clothing that allows free movement. For partner dances, avoid jewelry that might snag clothing.

Proper Footwear

Clean, indoor-only dance shoes are required. For ballet, wear ballet slippers. For partner dances, wear dance shoes. Street shoes are not permitted on the dance floor.

Stay Hydrated

Bring a water bottle to stay hydrated during class. No food or drinks (except water) are allowed in the studio.

Change Shoes at Reception

Please change into your dance shoes at the reception area before entering the class. This helps keep our dance floors clean and in good condition.

Registration Form

Be sure to sign the Registration Form before the class begins.

We Look Forward to Seeing You in Class!

At Aneska Dance School, our mission is to provide a fun, supportive, and enriching experience for all students.
